This project aimed to empower visually impaired individuals through a mental health workshop centered on art therapy. The creative artworks that participants expressed themselves were then transformed into designs for merchandise (T-shirt), which will be sold to raise awareness about inclusivity and generate funds to support disabled communities.

Mission & Vision

Mission

NEXUS is dedicated to fostering connections among individuals and communities through shared values of inclusivity, empowerment, and creativity. We strive to create a positive impact by reinvesting our profits into resources and support for vulnerable communities, ensuring that every purchase contributes to a brighter future for all.

Vision

Our vision at NEXUS is to be a transformative force for social good, where every person feels valued and empowered. We envision a world where communities thrive through collaboration and creativity, breaking down barriers and building a sustainable future for all, driven by our commitment to inclusivity and social responsibility.
